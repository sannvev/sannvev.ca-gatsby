## Mission

The Institute advances the science and practice of **auditable information systems** — with a particular focus on multi-agent AI, where questions of "who knows what, when, and on what evidence" are no longer theoretical but operationally decisive.

Our work bridges formal epistemic logic, large language models, and the governance frameworks under which both are deployed. We produce research, prototypes, and partnerships that make advanced AI systems legible to the institutions that depend on them.

## Governance

The Sannvev Institute is incorporated as a Canadian federal not-for-profit corporation under the *Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act*, with its registered office in Winnipeg, Manitoba. The Institute is a member of **Manitoba Innovates**, part of the province's innovation ecosystem.

The Institute operates under a director-led governance model. It does not solicit public donations at this time; inquiries regarding collaboration, sponsored research, and grant partnerships are welcomed via [Contact](/contact/).
