## Government

The Institute welcomes engagement with Canadian and allied government programs on auditable AI, information provenance, and related areas of defence and cyber innovation. Typical forms of engagement include:

- Briefings and technical consultations with program offices and procurement staff.
- Participation in challenge-based innovation programs and prize competitions.
- Joint research agreements in collaboration with university partners.

## Industry

The Institute partners with industry on research problems that are unlikely to be solved inside any single company — problems of standardization, auditability, and cross-boundary trust. Engagement forms include sponsored research, consortium participation, and focused advisory work.

## Academia

The Institute works with academic researchers — graduate students, faculty, and research centres — whose work addresses the Institute's research agenda. We support collaborative grant applications where academic leadership is required, and provide industrial context for academic research programs.

## Inquiries

All engagement inquiries may be directed to the Institute via [Contact](/contact/). Please include a brief description of the proposed engagement, any applicable program or funding context, and a point of contact.
